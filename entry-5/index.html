Noguchi Museum, Long Island City, Queens




Housed in an old brick industrial building in Long Island City, the Noguchi Museum houses the collection of the late 20th-century American Sculpture and Landscaping architect, Isamu Noguchi.

Fast Facts

The original industrial space house a photo etching compnay and their manufacturing equitment.

Within the original building, the open courtyard space and the galleries, each peice showcased works incollaboration with the site, making each work and the collection as a whole a unified environment in of itself.

Noguchi himself transformed the derelect courtyard into a minial, japanese inspired scultupre garden.

The collection includes over 60,000 works related to the artist life.
