Welcome to the Harmonic Collection, a curated selection of architectural marvels and cultural landmarks that define the essence of urban landscapes. Each entry in this collection offers a glimpse into the rich history, innovative design, and unique stories behind some of the most iconic structures around the world. Whether you're an architecture enthusiast, a history buff, or simply curious about the built environment, we invite you to explore and appreciate the harmony between form, function, and cultural significance that these landmarks embody.


3"x6" from 20ft.



The inspiration and story behind this collection comes from all the iphone pictures I take of facades of the buildings around me in Manhattan and the greater city. The title "3 inch x 6 inch from 20 feet" is meant to be a corrolation between the size of an iphone and the average distance that I tend to photograph these sites, usually 20 feet away- across the street.
